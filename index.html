Welcome to Towers of Hanoi!

How to play? Simple: move each disc onto the final skyscraper!

Rules
1) You may only place a disc on top of a larger disc.
2) You may only move one disc at a time.
3) You will be timed and the number of moves you take will be tracked. Aim for the smallest number of moves and shortest time possible.

You can change the number of discs by clicking the "Discs" button.

If you need to take a break, pause the game. If you'd like to start over, click "Restart". To see your highest score for the session, click "Score".

When you're ready to start, click "Start a New Game" at the top of the screen!

Have fun!
